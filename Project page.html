Project Details

You are a cursed adventurer who has come to an old castle in hopes of finding a way to lift their curse. Be wary, as there are traps, puzzles and ghosts that will try to stop you from freeing yourself from thes curse.

Trial of Time is an homage to an old flash game called 'Dungeon Run', in which there is a clock which you must bring 'minutes' to continue playing and eventually win the game.

The game was made over the course of 6 months in Unity.

Responsibilities

During the project I had taken the role of Lead programmer, developing the following features:

  • UI
  • Player movement
  • Lock and Key Mechanisms
  • Pick up and drop
  • Traps
  • Camera movement
  • Animated doors
  • Particle effects

Additionally, I was in charge of managing version control for the project and reviewing the code of the other programmer on the team.

Removing Doomer mindset...

Anyway, the announcement I wanted make is that I will be trying to add in an art site or something of the sort. It was always planned but this website was a small scale thing that I didn't really want to work on much unless I made significant progress. Which, I did by the way, so far I've progressed quite well (in my opnion) in my artistic skills, being able to create somewhat believable anthro-dragon characters. The presence of 'art pieces' (WIPs that I will either never finish or revisit sometime in the future) has facilitated the need for a platform to host them on. Now, I could always host them on deviantart, furaffinity and whatever other image/art hosting websites there are... but why? I don't wanna do that, I'm on those sites to browse not host (hypocritical, heh). I find it more interesting to develop my own personal little shitbox platform that's mine and mine alone, one which I know damn well what went into it, how it works and that my data ain't being held god-knows-where.

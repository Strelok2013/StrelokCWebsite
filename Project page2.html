Project Details

You play as Patrick C. Edderson, a cute stuffed bear that must survive endless waves of crazed stuffed animals trying to tear your stuffing out! Use your mighty scissor blade to cut them down and collect their buttons!
(Beware the Frankenstuffy!)

The game features gameplay similar to Diablo 3's combat minus the rpg elements. The overall theme of the game is inspired by games like Little big planet and Yoshi's wooly world.

Developed in Unity over the course of 6 months.

Responsibilities

I was appointed lead programmer for this project, developing the following features:

  • Power ups
  • Collectibles
  • Enemy AI
  • Enemy attacks
  • Player movement and look

In addition to managing version control for the project and communicating between the designers and artists.
